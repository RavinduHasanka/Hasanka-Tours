About Us

Welcome to Hasanka Tours

It's time to start your adventure

Sri Lanka, an island nation in the Indian Ocean, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," it boasts diverse landscapes, from lush tea plantations in the central highlands to pristine beaches along its coastline. Key attractions include ancient cities like Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, the picturesque city of Kandy with its sacred Temple of the Tooth, and the wildlife-rich Yala National Park. Tourists flock to Sri Lanka for its vibrant festivals, exquisite cuisine, and opportunities for adventure, including surfing, hiking, and wildlife safaris. The country offers a unique blend of cultural experiences and natural wonders, making it a captivating destination for travelers.
